Safety Announcement

Updated Safety Protocols for On-site Operations 2024

HR Compliance Department

Oct 24, 2023 • 5 min read • 1.2k Views

PT Prima Sarana Gemilang is committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety for all employees. Following the annual audit by the Occupational Health and Safety Committee, we are rolling out updated protocols for 2024.

1. Mandatory PPE Upgrades

Starting from January 1st, all on-site personnel must utilize the new Grade-A reflective vests and smart helmets equipped with proximity sensors. These sensors are integrated with the site's beacon system to prevent vehicle-related accidents.

2. Revised Heat Stress Management

Given the projected increase in temperature for the upcoming dry season, rest intervals have been adjusted. Mandatory hydration breaks of 15 minutes are now required every 2 hours of direct sun exposure. Cooling stations will be installed across sectors A through D.

Conclusion

All staff members are required to read the full policy attached in the documents section. Failure to adhere to these new protocols by the effective date may result in site access restrictions. We appreciate your cooperation in keeping our workplace safe.
