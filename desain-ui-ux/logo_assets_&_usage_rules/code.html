Minimum Clearance

Safe Zone
LOGO

Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to 50% of the logo height. This ensures visibility and prevents the logo from being cluttered by other elements or text.

  • No text inside the safe zone
  • No UI elements or photography overlap
