SAFETY PROTOCOL & COMPLIANCE

1. Objective

The objective of this protocol is to establish a comprehensive framework for health, safety, and environmental protection across all operational sites of PT Prima Sarana Gemilang. This document outlines the mandatory requirements for personnel safety, equipment operation, and incident reporting.

2. Scope

This protocol applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors engaged in operations at any facility owned or managed by PT Prima Sarana Gemilang. Compliance is mandatory and is a condition of employment/contract.

3. Mandatory Protective Equipment (PPE)

Standard site PPE includes, but is not limited to: Hard hats, high-visibility vests, steel-toed boots, and safety glasses. Specialized operations (e.g., welding, chemical handling) require additional equipment as specified in the PPE Matrix Appendix B.

4. Incident Reporting

All incidents, including "near misses," must be reported immediately to the site supervisor and documented via the internal E-Incident portal within 24 hours of occurrence. Failure to report may result in disciplinary action.
